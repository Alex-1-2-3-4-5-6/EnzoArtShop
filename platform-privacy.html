Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

COPPA Compliance: Little Heartworks is committed to protecting children's privacy. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All accounts are created and managed by parents or guardians.

1. Who We Are

Little Heartworks is an online platform where parents and guardians can share and sell artwork created by their children. We take privacy seriously, especially when it comes to protecting young people.

2. Who Can Create an Account

Only parents and guardians (adults 18 or older) may create accounts on Little Heartworks. We do not allow children under 13 to create accounts or submit personal information directly.

3. Information We Collect from Parents

When a parent creates an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Date of birth (to verify you are an adult)
  • Password (stored securely, never visible to us)
  • Optional profile photo

4. Information We Do NOT Collect from Children

We do not collect any personal information from children, including:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Photos of children themselves
  • Location data
  • Any other identifying information

Artwork submitted to the platform is uploaded and managed entirely by the parent.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display approved artwork in the public shop
  • To process purchase requests via email
  • To communicate with you about your account

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

6. Artwork in the Shop

When artwork is approved and listed in the public shop, the artwork title and price are publicly visible. No personal information about the child artist is displayed. Parents control what is listed and can remove artwork at any time from their dashboard.

7. Data Storage

Account and artwork data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database platform. Images are stored in Supabase Storage. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

8. Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review all information associated with your account
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Remove any artwork from the shop at any time
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Cookies

We use session cookies only to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request deletion of your data, please contact us at: pilotal2020@gmail.com
